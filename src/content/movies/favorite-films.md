---
title: "My All-Time Favorite Films"
---

## 1. The Shawshank Redemption

- **Year**: 1994
- **Rating**: 10/10
- **Review**: A masterpiece about hope, friendship, and perseverance. Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman deliver unforgettable performances in this timeless classic.

## 2. Inception

- **Year**: 2010
- **Rating**: 9/10
- **Review**: Christopher Nolan's mind-bending thriller that explores dreams within dreams. The practical effects and Hans Zimmer's score are phenomenal.

## 3. Spirited Away

- **Year**: 2001
- **Rating**: 10/10
- **Review**: Hayao Miyazaki's enchanting masterpiece. A beautiful coming-of-age story set in a magical bathhouse. The animation and imagination are unparalleled.

## 4. The Grand Budapest Hotel

- **Year**: 2014
- **Rating**: 9/10
- **Review**: Wes Anderson's whimsical and visually stunning film. Perfect symmetry, pastel colors, and an engaging story with incredible performances.

## 5. Parasite

- **Year**: 2019
- **Rating**: 10/10
- **Review**: Bong Joon-ho's brilliant social commentary disguised as a thriller. Masterful storytelling that keeps you guessing until the very end.
